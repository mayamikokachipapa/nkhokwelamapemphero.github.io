Nkhokwe la Mapemphero

Takulandirani ku tsamba lino la Nkhokwe la mapemphero. Mu nkhokwemu, mupeza mapemphero, kusinkhasinkha, ndi zina zotero zokhudza chikhulupiliro cheni cheni cha Katolika. Cholinga cha tsamba lino ndikukondwelera ukuya ndi kuzama kwa zomwe m'pingo wa Katolika umaphunzitsa, zomwe zasungidwa m'chiyankhulo cha chichewa, komanso kuyesetsa kuti zolembedwa zina zambiri zipezekenso m'chiyankhulo cha chichewa.
