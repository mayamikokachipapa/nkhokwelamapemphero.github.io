Kusinkhasinkha Nyimbo ya Oyera Onse

Oyera Mtima By Fr. Mayamiko Kachipapa, SJ Mu chimangirizo ichi, ndisinkhasinkha za mawu ndi nyimbo No. 180 mu Buku la Nyimbo ndi Mapemphero, yotchedwa”Oyera Mtima”. Mawu: Oyera Mtima Oyera mtima inu muli m'paradizo chimwemwe muli nacho kodi n'chachikulu? Chikondwerero chathu simungadziwitse pakukhala ndi Mulungu tili nazo zonse Nonsenu mudanyoza ulemu pansi dziko lanulo ndani akutama inu? Yesu atigawira za Ufumu wake ndi ulemu tikhala pafupi ndi Iye Inu mudasauka monga Mbuye Yesu kodi mumvabe njala, chisanu ndi ludzu? Ambuye Yesu yemwe atisekeretsa zovuta zonse zija zatha ndithu konse. Koma tikufunsani kumwamba kwa Mulungu chikondwerero chanu chimaliza liti? Chimwemwe chathu n'chosamalizika mazunzo akalero chitiyiwalitsa Inu mudala m'mwamba mutiloze njira yofikitsa kwa Mulungu, tikakondwe nanu Mukafuna kufika komwe tili ife mudane ndi Satana ndi zoyipa zake Abwenzi a Mulungu, titama inu mutipempheretu kwa Ambuye Yesu Abale okondeka, tipempha Mulungu kuti mukasekere inunso kumwamba. Mu nyimbo iyi tikukumana ndi macheza (dialogue) ongoganizira, kucheza pa mkumano wa Oyera ndi ife anthu a pa dziko pano. Tingati akumana maso ndi maso kaya titi pa tebulo, kaya titi pa bawo, kaya pa mpando. Tsono poti pali mbali ziwiri zochita zoyankhulana, nditchula oyimirila ife anthu a padziko lino kuti "munthu" pamene "Oyera" akhala oyimilira Oyera onse. Tiona kuti nyimboyi yagawidwa mwa njira ziwiri. Nnjira yoyamba yakagawidwe, tiona kuti verse iliyonse ili ndi mizere iwiri imene itsegulira verse yomwe ikupereka mawu athu, kupereka funso lathu kwa Oyera omwe ali kumvetsera, ndipo Iwo apereka yankho lawo mu mizere iwiri yotsatira ya verse yomweyo. Kagawidwe kachiwiri ndi kokhudza ma verse ndipo kakuwonetsa kuzama kwa munthu pamene alikumvetsera kwa Oyera. Tiona kuti pa ma verse six omwe apanga nyimboyi, mafunso a mu ma verse anayi alikupereka mafunso ongosonyeza kuzizwa (curiosity), pamene ma verse awiri otsiriza munthu awoneka kuti wazama ndithu mu zolinga zake, ndipo pano alikufunsa Oyera kuti nanga iye angachite bwanji kuti akhalenso naye Oyera. Kotsirizira kweni kweni, Oyera amutsogolera munthu mu pemphero. Choncho tiona kuti nyimboyi yapangidwa kuti ithere mu pemphero. Tikawerenganso ma verse aja kachiwiri, tiona kuti momwe ma verse aja ayendera, munthu ali kuvutika ndi kumvetsa za chimwemwe chomwe Oyera ali nacho. Munthuyu ali ngati ife tonse, ali kuona kuti Oyera ali osilirika koma akukayikira ngati alidi osangalala monga ife nthawi zina tisangalalira. Oyera atiyankha kuti sitingayambe mkomwe kumvetsa chimwemwe chomwe Oyera ali nacho ndipo apereka mawu amphamvu zedi: pakukhala ndi Mulungu, tili nazo zonse. Zitanthauza chani? Ife anthu zikondwerero zathu pano pa dziko kawirikawiri zimakhala kuyesetsa kwathu kuti tichepetse mavuto kapena nkhawa (masiku ano timati kudzipepesa). Timachita chikondwerero pamene tiona chinthu chosilirika chomwe chachitika, monga chikwati kapena graduation kapena mwina tapeza ndalama zoposera momwe tinayembekezera. Mu zonsezi, tiona kuti zolinga zathu pa chikondwerero si chikondwererocho chabe kuti zolinga zathu zimakhala kusangalalira kugonjetsa zovuta. Tsono, Oyera atiuza kuti iwo kumwamba pakukhala ndi Mulungu ali nazo zonse. Palibe zovuta, palibe kuchepekedwa, kulibe nkhawa. Chikondwerero chawo ndi cheni cheni popeza iwo sali kungoyesera kuchepetsa mavuto monga momwe ife tichitira pa chikondwerero. Potisonyeza izi, atimema kuti tiyesetse tikafikeko. Atasunthika, tiona munthu uja akufunsa Oyera uja kuti aloze njira. Ndiponso pamapeto amuthandiza kupemphera. Nyimbo yamba ngati macheza omwe athera ngati pemphero ndi chiganizo cha mum’tima
