Zokhudza nkhokweyi

Cholinga cha tsamba lino ndikukondwelera maphunziro akuya ndi ozama omwe m'pingo wa Katolika umaphunzitsa, ndipo wakhala ukuphunzitsa kufumira kwa Apostoli, zomwe zasungidwa m'chilankhulo cha Chichewa.

Cholinga chachiwiri ndi kuyesetsa kuti zolembedwa zina zambiri zipezekenso m'chilankhulo cha Chichewa potanthauzira zolemba za Aphunzitsi a Mpingo aja amatchedwa pa chingerezi kuti Church Fathers.
